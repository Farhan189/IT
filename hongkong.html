FAMOUS PLACES IN HongKong TO VISIT

01 Ocean Park

Ocean Park is a marine life theme park based on animals, rides, and shows. Covering an area about 91.5 hectares, Ocean Park in Hong Kong is located in the southern part of the island.
It was opened in 1977

02 DisneyLand

The worldwide famous theme park, Disneyland is located in Penny's Bay, Lantau Island.
It is located inside the Hong Kong Disneyland Resort, and is a dream place to visit for all kids.
From children to adults, everyone loves this dream place which is full Hong Kong Disneyland rides, cartoon characters, and magical amusement.

03 Big Buddha

The Big Buddha statue, Tian Tan Buddha, is a major crowd-puller for tourists from all over the world, be it a pilgrim or any other holidaymaker.
This statue of Buddha Shakyamuni is made of bronze and 34 meters tall. The statue weighs nearly 250 metric tons.
It is located on Ngong Ping Lantau Island, and is connected to the mainland via Big Buddha Hong Kong cable cars which are the best means of transport to reach this religious place.

04 Macau Tower

Featuring thrilling adventure activities and stunning panoramic views of the Macau city and the ocean, the Macau Tower is a must-visit spot for all those who wish to enjoy Macau to its fullest.
More than 300 meters in height this tower is one of the most eye-catching sights on the night skyline of the city.
With some thrilling activities on the top few floors of the Macau Tower, the lower levels are decked with world-class restaurants and shopping plazas where you can find almost everything in high-end fashion and accessories.
